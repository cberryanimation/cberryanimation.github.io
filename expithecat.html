About Me

I am ExpitheCat. I have created this website from scratch (via HTML and CSS code) to serve as my portfolio.

Above you will find my demo reel, containing some art pieces as well as spliced footage from previous animations I have done.

I am currently going to college for an Associate's Degree in Animation and certificate in Web Design. I have a lot of experience with character design as seen in a lot of my animation and art work. I enjoy creating characters and writing stories about them.

On the note of creating characters, I am in the process of writing my own series about them in addition to creating a webcomic. Further information on that may be added here in the future.
