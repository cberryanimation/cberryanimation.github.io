Sodor Railway Friends (2021)

Sodor Railway Friends (2021) is a stopmotion Thomas Wooden Railway series that I have been planning for a little while. This more or less serves as a reboot or continuation of another Thomas Wooden Railway series I created in 2014 but cancelled after 8 episodes. If you read my Expi's Adventure page, this is a similar situation where the state of the project and my work on it ultimately comes down to how much time I have and how much I'd like to put that towards other projects. So far I have written some stories and plan to start this series in at least the first half of 2021.
